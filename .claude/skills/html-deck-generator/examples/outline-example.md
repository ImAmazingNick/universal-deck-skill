# Example Intent Plan → Slide Outline

**User Prompt:** "Create an HTML deck pitching our AI agents platform to enterprise COOs. Focus on cost savings, reliability, and roadmap."  
**Audience:** Enterprise operations leaders  
**Tone:** Confident, data-driven, visionary  
**Goal:** Secure follow-up demo call  
**Desired CTA:** "Schedule a strategy session"

## Slide Strategy

1. **Hero** – Vision statement, key stats (hero layout)
2. **Problem** – Operational inefficiencies (stats grid)
3. **Opportunity** – Market momentum (metrics grid)
4. **Solution Overview** – AI agent architecture (feature showcase)
5. **Workflow Demo** – Step-by-step pipeline (content slide with diagram)
6. **ROI Proof** – Before/after comparison (comparison container)
7. **Customer Success** – Case study snippet + testimonial
8. **Roadmap** – 12-month rollout (timeline)
9. **Pricing & Packaging** – Simple summary (content slide)
10. **CTA** – Strategy session invite (cta-section)

Use this outline to populate the HTML template, duplicating/adjusting slides as necessary.
